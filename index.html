Parking Lot Gates at the BOplex

The parking lot of the BOplex will be closed from 6:30 PM to 6:00 AM weekdays

When there is an event, the hours may be changed. The CRVA staff may provide information at any time to have these hours revised. During events, the Parking staff will usually attend to opening the gates but closing them will probably fall to Allied. The decision on when to close gates may depend on the number and location of vehicles in the parking area.
Overhead View of the BOplex

Gate 1 - Ovens Front Entrance
Pull both gates closed.

Gate 2 - Ovens Side Exit from Pick Up Area
Pull the gate closed.
Place the DO NOT ENTER a-frame sign in front of the gate.

Stage Gate - Beside Ovens Side Exit from Pick Up Area
Put the barricade across the entrance.

Gate 3 - Parking Lot Entrance Behind Ovens
Pull both sides of the gate closed.

Gate 4 - Ovens Far Parking Lot Entrance
Pull the left (downhill) side of the gate closed.
Place cones to block the rest of the opening.

Gate 5 - Coliseum Parking Entrance from Briar Creek Road
Pull both gates together.

Gate 6 - Coliseum Lower Level
Normally this is left open. However, if instructed, put the baricades across the driveway.
Place the DO NOT ENTER a-frame sign in the middle or at the right end.

Gate 7 - Coliseum Remote Lot Entrance
Normally this is left open. However, if instructed, pull the two gates together.
